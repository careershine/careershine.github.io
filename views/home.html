Welcome To Career Shine Academy


Career Shine Academy, as an education partner of Hughes Global Education, offers executive programs directly from IIMs, IITs, and other prestigious institutions. Career Shine Academy is partnership firm established on May 2015 at East Tambaram- Chennai. Main objective of Career Shine Academy is to provide the best B-schools executive programmes for the professionals residing around Tambaram -Chennai Circle.


Hughes Global Education is the pioneer in providing the ‘Interactive Online Learning Platform’ for the working professionals to pursue an executive mba. The programs are provided by the top notch B-schools viz.IIM Calcutta, IIM Kozhikode, IIM Rohtak, IIFT New Delhi and XLRI Jamshedpur. The best faculties in the country take live lectures imparting knowledge that is exclusive with specializations across various streams like General Management, Sales & Marketing, Human Resource, Project Management, Finance, International Trade etc
