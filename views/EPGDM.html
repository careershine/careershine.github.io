Executive Post Graduate Diploma in Management (EPGDM)


Overview

Executive Post Graduate Diploma in Management (EPGDM) is a two-year post graduate diploma programme offered on Interactive Onsite Learning (IOL) platform. The objective of the programme is to impart management education to working executives seeking skills and strategies to take their organizations to the next level.The course with over 840 classroom instruction is designed to cater to the requirements of a cross-section of industry, enabling participants to share their rich and diverse experiences among themselves in addition to having the classroom learning. The Programme intends to enable its participants to :

  • Broaden and enrich their managerial skills to leverage on the growing opportunities.
  • Enhance their analytical abilities in tune with the evolving business complexities.
  • Develop a comprehensive and holistic understanding of business management and its varied functions to attain professional success commensurate with the full individual potential.
  • Acquire decisional and leadership capabilities required for their career growth and professional progress against the changing business environment and growing global integration.
  • Strengthen strategic thinking skills so as to become and remain successful top managers and CEOs.

Between 2-3 days every week as per the schedule mutually decided. Exceptions shall be mutually agreed upon in case of scheduling difficulties.

Duration

2 Years

Pedagogy

  • Theory & Practical Inputs.
  • Case study approach.
  • Assignments, E-Learning, & Business Simulation.

Eligibility

  • Applicants should be working professionals/self-employed.
  • Graduates (10+2+3) / Post Graduates in any discipline with min. 50% marks [aggregate - considering results of all years (e.g. 3 or 4 together)] recognized by UGC/AICTE.
  • Minimum 3 years of work experience (full-time paid employment) post completion of graduation as on Application Closure Date.

Selection Criteria

  • Retail Applications + credentials and Corporate Nominations
  • Applicants will be selected based on performance in the Entrance Test and interviews
  • Selection will be: i) Highly Selective ii) Selective iii) Moderate

Who Should Attend:

  • Those wanting to build a career in management: working executives keen on paying a higher price for the brand
  • The programme offers quality interactions with experienced executives, and peer group learning.
