Post Graduate Certificate in Business Management (PGCBM)


Overview

The need for management education in today’s world for effective performance is a foregone conclusion. While experience might have been the backbone of effective performance in the corporate world of yesterday, knowledge and continuous learning is the mainstay amongst the best organizations of today. In order to satisfy this thirst for knowledge and to become a learning organization, XLRI has evolved a 12 months consolidated program in Business Management.

Course Objective

To give participants an understanding of problems and issues in the field of Business and Management

To give the participants a holistic perspective of Business Management so that they can shoulder greater responsibilities in their careers ahead with better awareness.

The latest and the best practices and problem solving acumen from the Indian as well as International context

To provide skills, knowledge and tactical approach to assess the business situations and handle tasks more effectively.

Programme Objectives

Premier certification from XLRI
Career Enhancement

Who Should Attend

Executives and professionals in business organizations may apply for this program. The program offers quality interactions with experienced executives, and peer group learning as average experience of the participants is around 7 years.

Eligibility

Applicants should be working professionals/self-employed.
Must possess minimum of 2 years of work experience in related field after completing graduation.
Full Time Degree Graduates (10+2+3) in any discipline, recognized by UGC/AICTE.

Admission Criteria & Profile Desired

Academic Record
Years and Quality of work experience.
Personal Interviews to be conducted by XLRI faculty using Hughes Global Education Interactive Onsite Learning Platform

Candidates who qualify the above eligibility criteria will be selected into the program. No deviations to the laid out Eligibility Criteria will be considered. The desired candidate for the program would ideally have a good work experience (around 5 years) with a strong academic background. Proficiency in English is desired since it would be the medium of instruction. The candidate must also have the drive to learn and imbibe the teachings of the program. General knowledge of the industry of work and working knowledge of computers is also necessary.

Core Value

  • Recognized certification coming from a reputed institute like XLRI
  • XLRI is among the top 10 Indian B-schools & a pioneer in starting the program of this kind not only in India but in Asia-Pacific.
  • Over 4000 working professionals have benefited so far from this program of XLRI - participants have advanced their careers within and outside their organizations.
  • One of the most rigorous and quality online programs in the country in management
  • Quality interactions with experienced executives and peer group learning as average experience of the participants in last few batches have been 5+ years
  • Campus component of one week in the beautiful and advanced XLRI Campus in Jamshedpur.
  • Specifically designed for working executives with all the flexibility benefits of Interactive Onsite Learning.
  • Participants selected based on elaborate selection process including SOP, academic performance, job responsibility and interviews etc.

Certification

The examination and evaluation required for certification shall be carried out XLRI Jamshedpur. The faculty members teaching their respective courses will choose the method of evaluation most appropriate to the material. The certificate will be awarded directly by XLRI Jamshedpur. Successful Candidates will be offered Alumni status.
